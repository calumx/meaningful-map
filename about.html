About

The Meaningful Map of Glasgow is, and always will be, non-commercial. It was not commissioned by any company or public organisation and so has got no hidden agenda beyond curious exploration.

Rather, it starts as a personal project that will hopefully develop with your help into a fully collaborative effort. If there is a community appetite, I would be keen to open this up for workshops and a dedicated forum. Ideally we will all decide the course of the project and its final form together.

There are currently no associated organisations.
All contributors will be informed of any signifcant future plans or potential collaborations. However, no data will ever be sold to any private company.
