How will we do it?

1. Remote conversation

We’ll kick off with remote chats, facilitated personally by me. No need to prepare as I will guide you through your memories.

2. Walking and talking

When the lockdown restrictions lift we will finally be able to meet face to face, to discover the city through your unique perspective.

3. Finalising the story

You will have a final say on how your contribution is presented. We’ll collaborate as much as you choose. Ultimately, it’s your story.
